ABOUT THIS MARKET

The Associated Students Farmers Market at SF State operates on the SF State University campus on Thursdays. It is run through the university's Associated Students organization and serves primarily SFSU students, faculty, and staff, though it is open to the surrounding community. Campus farmers markets exist to make fresh produce accessible to students who may otherwise rely entirely on campus dining or convenience stores. The Lake Merced / Lakeshore neighborhood surrounding SFSU has limited walkable fresh food options, making this market a genuine food access point.

Vendor details and schedules may vary by semester. For current hours and vendor information, check with the SFSU Associated Students office or the campus events calendar.
