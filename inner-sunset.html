FEATURED VENDORS · SUNDAY MARKET
Life Food Gardens
Specialty sprouts & microgreens · Bay Area
▼
Life Food Gardens grows specialty sprouts and microgreens, and is one of the most distinctive vendors at the Inner Sunset market. Their selection rotates constantly — sunflower, pea, radish, broccoli, clover, wheatgrass, and specialty blends that change week to week. Sprouts and microgreens are harvested within days of sale, which means their freshness at the market exceeds anything available at grocery stores by a significant margin. SF Chronicle food writers specifically flag Life Food Gardens as an Inner Sunset market highlight.
WHAT THEY BRING
Sunflower sprouts Pea shoots Radish microgreens Broccoli sprouts Wheatgrass Seasonal specialty blends
Note: Selection varies weekly — ask what's freshest

Hummus Heaven
Housemade hummus & spreads · San Leandro, CA
▼
A PCFMA market staple, Hummus Heaven's Inner Sunset presence serves the neighborhood's dense population of young families and UC Medical Center staff. Fresh-made hummus without stabilizers, rotating flavors, baba ganoush, and fresh pita.
WHAT THEY BRING

Shelly's Farm Fresh
Pasture-raised eggs · Sonoma County
▼
Pasture-raised eggs from Sonoma County, where hens forage on open grass rotation. The yolks are noticeably more orange and flavorful than cage-free or free-range commercial eggs. A Sunday morning staple for Inner Sunset households that cook from scratch.

ABOUT THIS MARKET

The Inner Sunset Farmers Market has been operating since 2009 in a parking lot between 8th and 9th avenues, south of Irving Street — slightly hidden from casual passersby, but well-known to the neighborhood. It is operated jointly by PCFMA and the Inner Sunset Park Neighbors neighborhood organization, which gives it a dual character: professional market management with genuine neighborhood involvement. Kids activities and live music run most Sundays.
