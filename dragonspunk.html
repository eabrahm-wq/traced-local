ABOUT THIS MARKET

The Dragonspunk Farmers Market operates Thursday evenings in the Bayview-Hunters Point neighborhood at 1550 Evans Avenue — one of SF's most underserved communities for fresh food access. Bayview-Hunters Point has historically been a food desert: a densely populated neighborhood with very few grocery stores selling fresh produce at accessible prices, high rates of diet-related illness, and a predominantly Black and working-class population that has been systematically excluded from SF's food system investments.

Dragonspunk is a community-embedded market that operates with food justice as a core value rather than a tagline. The Thursday 3–7:30pm window serves residents who work and can't make weekend or early-morning markets. CalFresh EBT is accepted. For current vendor information and community events, verify directly via the market's local organizers.
