Annie’s

Homegrown Mac & Cheese · “Noodles & More Since 1989”
Traced lede
Annie’s was built as an organic alternative to Kraft. In 2014 it was acquired for $820M by General Mills—maker of Cheerios and Lucky Charms—while the “independent” aesthetic stayed intact.
