The red flag isn’t that acquisitions exist — it’s the continuation of independent-brand cues without making ownership legible to consumers in the primary experience (front-of-pack + marketing).

Evidence
2014 acquisition
General Mills buys Annie’s for ~$820M. Brand identity remains intact.
Mechanism
Values-washing
Maintain “homegrown” aesthetics while revenues flow to a conglomerate.
User impact
Choice illusion
Parents seeking “indie” may unknowingly fund the very CPG ecosystem they avoid.
